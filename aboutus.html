Our story

Rebel Lion is set up by three enthusiastic students from Breda University of Applied sciences with much passion and excitement. The brand Rebel Lion is set up by students, and for students

Because let's face it, being a student is not always fun. Being a student is all about learning, growing, having fun and managing to keep everything in line. There is much being asked from young adults, they are supposed to conform to the ideal image and give up a lot for this. We, Rebel Lion, want to break this image and tell the young that you can be yourself, live your best life, and we want to show that going out of your comfort zone is fun. Rebel Lion beer is a drink made to help you stand up for yourself, to experience new things and to take control of your own life. Because only then you will get to experience the student life that you have wished for.

Vision

In a society where the young are being taught to be followers and conform to the norms of society, we believe that only when you stand up for yourself, go out of your comfort zone and become your own person you will be able to take control of your own life.
