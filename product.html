Rebel Lion Original

Original Rebel Lion Taste

Rebel Lion Original. Not just a normal beer, but a beer that gives you a boost in energy, in joy and in rebellion. With a dark but sweet taste, 8.9% vol and a nice packaging, we are sure you will love this beer as much as we do. Brewed with passion in the Dutch brewery located in Breda. Enjoy a Rebel Lion original at home or in your favourite restaurant/pub and have a great time.
